## Classic Banana Bread

A moist and delicious banana bread that's perfect for using up overripe bananas.

## Ingredients

- 3 ripe bananas
- 1/3 cup melted butter
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 egg, beaten
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- Pinch of salt
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

## Instructions

1. Preheat your oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease a 4x8 inch loaf pan.
2. Mash the ripe bananas in a large mixing bowl until smooth.
3. Stir in the melted butter.
4. Mix in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract.
5. Sprinkle in the baking soda and salt, and mix well.
6. Add the flour and stir until just combined — don't overmix.
7. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan.
8. Bake for 55–60 minutes, until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
9. Let cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.

## Tips

- The riper the bananas, the sweeter and more flavorful your bread will be.
- You can add a handful of chocolate chips or walnuts to the batter.
