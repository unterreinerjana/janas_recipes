## Tomato Basil Pasta

A simple and fresh pasta dish ready in under 30 minutes.

## Ingredients

- 400g spaghetti
- 4 medium tomatoes, diced
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- Parmesan cheese for serving

## Instructions

1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water before draining.
2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
3. Add garlic and sauté for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
4. Add diced tomatoes and cook for 5–7 minutes, until they break down into a sauce.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Toss the cooked spaghetti in the pan, adding a splash of pasta water to loosen if needed.
7. Remove from heat, stir in fresh basil leaves.
8. Serve with grated Parmesan.

## Tips

- Use San Marzano tomatoes for the best flavor.
- A pinch of red pepper flakes adds a nice kick.
