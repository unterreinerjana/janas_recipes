## Greek Salad

A refreshing and vibrant salad full of Mediterranean flavors.

## Ingredients

- 2 large cucumbers, chopped
- 4 medium tomatoes, cut into wedges
- 1 red onion, thinly sliced
- 1 cup Kalamata olives
- 200g feta cheese, cubed or crumbled
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine cucumbers, tomatoes, and red onion in a large bowl.
2. Add the olives and feta cheese.
3. Drizzle with olive oil and red wine vinegar.
4. Sprinkle with dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
5. Toss gently and serve immediately.

## Tips

- Use full-fat block feta for the best texture and flavor.
- Let the salad sit for 10 minutes before serving to let the flavors meld.
